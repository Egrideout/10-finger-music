Our Story

About 10 Finger Music

My name is Eric Rideout and I am the founder of 10 Finger Music. I believe that music has the power to transform lives and create lasting memories. I started my music journey as a pre-teen playing in school band and at church. Fast forward 30+ years, having played in college bands, for choirs, on tour with blues artists, in the studio, formal instruction in classical and jazz, I continue my love for piano. I even shared that love with my kids by teaching them piano. My ultimate goal is to share the gift of music with others and help shape their musical journey.

Whether you're a curious beginner taking your very first steps on the keys, or you're planning an elegant event that deserves a stunning live soundtrack — I am here to make it extraordinary.
